We do not sell your data. It may be shared only with services necessary for operation (notifications, subscription management) subject to their own privacy policies.

4. Retention

Data is retained as long as your account is active or as technically required by the service.

5. Your Choices

You can modify your watchlist and preferences in the app. You may request account deletion by contacting us.
